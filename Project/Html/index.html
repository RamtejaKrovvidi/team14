Ingredients & Method


Flour,water,sea salt, and leavening:four simpe ingredients that-when mixed,shaped,fermented,and baked-make one of the most basic foods that has nourished humans for thousaands of years.Since such few ingredients are involved,two things set great bread apart:quality ingredients and time.
